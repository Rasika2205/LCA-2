Unexplored in Rajasthan
Kumbhalgarh Fort
Udaipur
Explore

Discover the grandeur of Kumbhalgarh Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Known for its massive walls and majestic architecture, it's a must-see for history and architecture enthusiasts.

Location: Udaipur, Rajasthan

Best Time to Visit: October - March

Jawai Bandh
Sumerpur
Explore

Jawai Bandh is famous for its scenic views and wildlife, especially the leopard sightings around the region. A unique blend of nature and wildlife, it's an offbeat destination in Rajasthan.

Location: Sumerpur, Rajasthan

Best Time to Visit: October - March

Bundi Palace
Bundi
Explore

Explore the Bundi Palace, a beautiful fusion of Rajput and Mughal architecture. Known for its intricate frescoes and carvings, the palace tells the story of Rajasthan's royal history.

Location: Bundi, Rajasthan

Best Time to Visit: October - March
